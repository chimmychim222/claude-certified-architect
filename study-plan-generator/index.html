How This Plan Is Built

The CCA Foundations exam weights its five domains unevenly — Agentic Architecture & Orchestration alone accounts for 27% of your score, more than the bottom two domains combined. This generator allocates your available study days in roughly the same proportions, so you spend time where the exam actually rewards it, rather than splitting your hours evenly across topics that don't carry equal weight.

If you flag a domain as a weak spot, its share of your schedule gets boosted by 50% (with the other domains trimmed slightly to make room) — so a domain you're shaky on gets meaningfully more attention without crowding out everything else. The last day or so of any plan is always reserved for full timed practice exams and targeted review, no matter how many weeks you have.

For the reasoning behind the domain weights and a full editorial walkthrough, see our 30-day study schedule. To find out where you currently stand before committing to a plan, try the free 10-question readiness diagnostic — it scores you across all five domains in about five minutes.
