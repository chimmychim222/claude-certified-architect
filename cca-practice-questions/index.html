How the Questions Work

When you start a practice session, questions are drawn from the full bank using a Fisher-Yates shuffle — every session presents a different order. You can choose from four practice modes:

  • Quick Sprint — 10 random questions in 20 minutes
  • Focused Session — 20 questions targeting core patterns in 40 minutes
  • Deep Practice — 30 questions across all domains in 60 minutes
  • Full Exam — 60 domain-weighted questions in 120 minutes

What Makes These Questions Effective

Scenario-Based Design

Every question places you in a realistic situation — you are the architect making a decision, not someone recalling a definition. This approach aligned with the exam format and builds transferable skills.

Detailed Explanations

After answering, you see a full breakdown: why the correct answer works, why the alternatives fail, and the underlying principle. This turns every mistake into a learning moment.

Domain-Weighted Scoring

Your results are scored with the same domain weights as the real CCA exam, so you see exactly where you stand in each area and where to focus additional study.
